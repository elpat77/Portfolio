About Me

Welcome to my site, I'm glad you stopped by!

I grew up in Mexico City and my dream was to become a professional Soccer Player (like most kids in Mexico). However, that dream came to an end after getting a knee injury. That's when I enrolled in College in order to study a carreer. I chose Hospitality Businesss Managemant since I'm a people person and are very passionate about leading teams.

Afer graduating, I opened and managed a Mexican/American fusion restaurant for 3 years, it was delicious! After selling the restaurant I moved to San Francisco and I've lived here ever since. I had the priviledge of managing some high end Restaurants and Banquet Facilities throughout the Bay Area, including The Top of the Mark at the Mark Hopkins Hotel and Bob's Steak & Chop House at The Omni Hotel.

One of the most valuable things I learned from Hospitality was the importance of always offering oustanding product quality and great customer experience. I believe these are the basics for any company to succeed!

Because of this and my love for technology, I decided to enroll in a Software Quality Assurance Program 7 years ago. I have to admit, it was pretty hard to transition into a new career, but it was best decission I ever made! In just a few years I've worked my way up to becoming a Sr. Software QA Engineer. During this time I have been working with some awesome products, great companies and very talented individuals.

I know that in order to succeed in this industry I have to keep learning and improving my skills. At the moment I'm enrolled in a Full Stack Developer Certificate Program at UC Berkeley Extension, gaining many skills needed to stay competitive and keep growing in this industry.

This website was built as part of a homework assignment, I hope you liked it!
